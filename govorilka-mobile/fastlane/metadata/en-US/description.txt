Govorilka is a minimalist app for converting speech to text using the advanced Deepgram Nova-2 AI.

WHY GOVORILKA?

• Free start — $200 credits when you sign up for Deepgram (≈775 hours of recording)
• No subscription — pay only for usage ($0.0043/min)
• Privacy — audio is not stored, history only on device
• Open source — full transparency on GitHub

FEATURES

◉ Instant Transcription
Speak — text appears in real time. WebSocket streaming technology ensures minimal latency.

◉ Smart Text Cleanup
Automatic removal of filler words. Enable with one tap in settings.

◉ Recording History
All transcriptions saved locally. Quick search, copy, and delete.

◉ Pro Mode
Take photos while recording. Perfect for feedback, bug reports, and contextual notes. Export to Google Drive.

◉ Sounds and Haptics
Tactile feedback during recording. Customizable sound notifications.

EXCELLENT SPEECH RECOGNITION

Deepgram Nova-2 is one of the best models for speech recognition. Higher accuracy than most competitors.

HOW TO START

1. Download the app
2. Get a free API key at deepgram.com
3. Paste the key in settings
4. Press the record button and speak!

PRIVACY

✓ Audio streams directly to Deepgram and is not saved
✓ History stored only on your device
✓ No analytics or tracking
✓ Source code open for review
